Two Worlds

Where the Lines Meet

On her father's side, Zoya descended from the princely houses of western Georgia — the Nakashidze of Guria, joined by marriage to the Mikeladze, Nizharadze, Marshania, Daniani, Tsulukidze, Shervashidze, Dadiani and Dadeshkeliani families: the great names of Guria, Samegrelo, Abkhazia and Svaneti. Her grandfather Mikhail Mikhailovich Nakashidze built the family house on Ninoshvili Street.

On her mother's side ran a British and Irish inheritance — the Sankey family with the O'Hara, Roe and Clarke lines of Ireland, and the Scottish Ponton, Nisbet and Patterson families. The two worlds met in the marriage of Prince Constantine Michael Nakashidze and Beatrice Josephine Villiers Sankey, and their daughter Zoya carried both.
